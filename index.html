Welcome to Mr Car Ltd

Mr Car Ltd. was established on 2007 as an independent Land Rover spare parts and accessories retailer and wholesaler through Online. From the very beginning we operated as a trustful, loyal and expert car part seller. Later in 2015 we started a trade counter. We now have the resources to sell these spare parts and accessories not only in UK but all over the world as per customer requirements by providing quality customer service and by maintaining the standard of this industry. Mr Car Ltd happy to announce we have open Bangladesh Branch which is company name Mr Car. Our Bangladeshis customer can buy online with BD Debit or credit card. Good thing there is no hidden price, UK & Bangladesh same price. We offer products to the customers with reasonable price in comparison with market. We also use Microcat, JEPC, Partlink24 and Daimler software's so that we can easily search parts for customers depending on which make of car they have.
